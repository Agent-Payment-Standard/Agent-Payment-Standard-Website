# Roadmap

The progression of APS is divided into four strategic phases.

## Phase 1: Preparation :check_mark_button:
Recruitment of core Technical Committee experts and initial standards framework research.

## Phase 2: Deliberation :construction:
Establishment of specialized task forces and public release of RFC drafts for community feedback.

## Phase 3: Publication :calendar:
Official release of the APS v0.1 core standard documentation, including technical specifications and legal guidelines.

## Phase 4: Ecosystem Growth :chart_increasing:
Launch of official reference implementation examples, developer tools, and compliance certification programs.
